Dr. Shaily R. Gandhi

A geospatial expert and problem-solver, I leverage spatial solutions to address complex urban challenges. My work focuses on collaborating with individuals from diverse fields to develop and implement innovative solutions for sustainable urban development.

I am currently a Postdoc at the Geo-social Analytics Lab at the Paris Lodron University of Salzburg. Previously, I served as Deputy Center Head of CAG, CRDF, and Program Chair of the M.Tech Geomatics program at CEPT University. My career has centered around bridging the gap between research and practical applications for real-world impact.

Throughout my career, I have led projects for esteemed clients, including the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs and GIZ, focusing on areas such as traffic management, mobility data harvesting, and sustainable urban development. I am passionate about promoting open data and capacity building, actively participating in Carpentries activities and leading the CODATA Connect working group.

As a recognized expert in my field, I am honored to be the recipient of awards including the International Science Council Fellowship for 2024 and to be recognized as a Geospatial World 50 Rising Star in 2023. I am deeply committed to nurturing the next generation of geospatial professionals and find immense fulfillment in contributing to research and innovation. Beyond my professional pursuits, I find joy in exploring new cultures through travel and connecting with individuals from all walks of life.
